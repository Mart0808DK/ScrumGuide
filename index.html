Scrum Guide Intro


Why SCRUM as a CS student?

“SCRUM is a way to work together as a team to create cool things! It's like a game plan for how to get things done. SCRUM breaks everything into small pieces and gives us a clear plan for what needs to be done and who's responsible for each piece. This helps us work faster, smarter, and more efficiently. Plus, it's a lot of fun!”

The Purpose and definition

  • Deliver high-quality products that meet customer needs
  • Increase productivity and efficiency
  • Manage and reduce project risk
  • Improve communication and transparency
  • Adapt to changing requirements and priorities
